About LOD Fashion Design

LOD Fashion Design specializes in creating customized clothing based on customer preferences. Our boutique offers unique and fashionable designs that blend creativity with quality.

Our Story

Founded in 2019 by Shraddha singh, LOD Fashion Design began as a small atelier in Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad. Over the years we have grown into a trusted boutique known for personalised service, exquisite tailoring and attention to detail.

Craft & Approach

Every garment is planned with the client — fabric selection, fit, silhouette and finishing. We combine modern cuts with artisanal techniques to create pieces that last beyond seasons.
